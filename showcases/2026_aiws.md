# RC4 Winter Break Closing Seminar 2026

Organized by Ho-min Park (Ph.D., Ghent University)

Running every semester since Winter 2023, the RC4 Seminar provides a collaborative space where IRTP and intern students present and discuss their ongoing research projects. This winter's closing seminar was held over two days, March 3rd and 4th, 2026, at Gh104 from 18:40 to 21:00.

The following table presents the list of IRTP supervisors and advisors for this winter's seminars:

| Supervisor | Advisor | Program Title |
|-----------|---------|--------------|
| Prof. Wesley De Neve & Prof. Joris Vankerschaver | Dr. Ho-min Park | RC4 Winter Break Closing Seminar 2026 |
